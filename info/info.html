About MaLT2

MALT2-ext - 'Machine Lab Turtlesphere-Extended' was developed as an extended version of MaLT2 application .
MaLT2-ext extends MaLT2 with the functionality of 3D printing of the digial models.
Its current version has been designed and developed by Prof. C.Kynigos and M. Grizioti and was funded by the Horizon Europe Extendt2 project

The currnet version uses the open-source js libraries three.js, jueryUI.js v.1.10 and codemirror.js v.4.0.
The commands of the developed Logo programming Language are based on the Berkeley Logo
The developed logo interpreter was based on the files 'papert.js' and 'logo.js' from the opensource project 'papert' that is released with an open source MITLicence
